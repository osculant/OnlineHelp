Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

01 Introduction

At **OnlineHelp**, we take your digital security seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our AI Security Scanner and inquiry forms. By using our services, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

02 Information We Collect

Form Data

When you request a report, we collect your full name, mobile number, email address, and system type. This is used solely to provide the requested security analysis.

Scan Meta-Data

Our simulation scan does not access private files. We only collect basic browser environment data to demonstrate potential vulnerabilities to the user.

03 How We Use Your Data

  • To generate and deliver your personalized security report via email.
  • To provide technical support and security updates regarding your specific system type.
  • To improve our AI detection algorithms through anonymized threat patterns.

04 Data Protection

We implement **AES-256 bit encryption** for all data transmitted through our forms. Your information is stored on secured servers and is never sold to third-party marketing agencies.

Have questions about your data?

You have the right to request data deletion at any time.
